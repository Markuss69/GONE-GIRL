Brief summary:

"Gone Girl" tells the story of Nick and Amy Dunne, a seemingly perfect couple who relocate from New York City to Nick's hometown in Missouri. On the day of their fifth wedding anniversary, Amy goes missing, and Nick becomes the prime suspect in her disappearance. As the police investigation unfolds, secrets are revealed, and the true nature of their relationship comes to light.
